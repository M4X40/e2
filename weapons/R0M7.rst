Ability Description
-------------------

Stormbreaker has the ability to Awaken. Every time you hit a mob, you will gain 1 pip (segment) of the awaken meter. Once full, right click to strike yourself with lightning, damaging and knocking back nearby mobs, awakening Stormbreaker.

While awakened, you will throw Stormbreaker instead of swinging it, and every hit on a mob has a 45% chance to strike them with lightning. After 5 seconds, Stormbreaker will go back to its Dormant form.
